Past Simple - Regular Verbs

🎯 ¿Cuándo usamos el pasado simple?

  • Acciones completadas en el pasado: I worked yesterday
  • Eventos específicos: She visited Paris last year
  • Rutinas pasadas: When I was young, I played soccer
  • Secuencia de eventos: I woke up, had breakfast, and went to work

📝 Formación del Pasado Simple Regular

✅ Regla General: Verbo + ED

Ejemplos básicos:

  • work → worked
  • play → played
  • watch → watched
  • listen → listened
  • walk → walked
  • talk → talked
  • cook → cooked
  • clean → cleaned

💡 Importante:

El pasado simple regular es igual para todas las personas (I, you, he, she, it, we, they)

🔤 Reglas Especiales de Ortografía

1. Verbos terminados en -E: Solo agregar -D

  • live → lived
  • like → liked
  • dance → danced
  • arrive → arrived

2. Verbos terminados en consonante + Y: Cambiar Y por -IED

  • study → studied
  • try → tried
  • cry → cried
  • carry → carried

3. Verbos terminados en vocal + Y: Solo agregar -ED

  • play → played
  • stay → stayed
  • enjoy → enjoyed

4. Verbos de una sílaba: Consonante + Vocal + Consonante

Duplicar la última consonante + ED

  • stop → stopped
  • plan → planned
  • shop → shopped

🔊 Pronunciación de -ED

Tres sonidos diferentes:

1. /t/ - Sonido T

Después de sonidos sordos: p, k, f, s, sh, ch

  • worked /wɜːrkt/
  • stopped /stɑːpt/
  • watched /wɑːtʃt/
  • laughed /læft/

2. /d/ - Sonido D

Después de sonidos sonoros: b, g, v, z, m, n, l, r y vocales

  • played /pleɪd/
  • lived /lɪvd/
  • cleaned /kliːnd/
  • arrived /əˈraɪvd/

3. /ɪd/ - Sonido ID

Después de sonidos T y D

  • wanted /ˈwɑːntɪd/
  • needed /ˈniːdɪd/
  • decided /dɪˈsaɪdɪd/
  • visited /ˈvɪzɪtɪd/

❌ Forma Negativa

Estructura: Sujeto + DIDN'T + Verbo base

Importante: Con DIDN'T, el verbo principal va en forma base (sin -ED)

Ejemplos:

✅ Correcto:
  • I didn't work yesterday
  • She didn't study last night
  • We didn't play soccer
  • They didn't arrive on time
❌ Incorrecto:
  • I didn't worked yesterday
  • She didn't studied last night
  • We didn't played soccer
  • They didn't arrived on time

❓ Forma Interrogativa

Estructura: DID + Sujeto + Verbo base?

Importante: Con DID, el verbo principal va en forma base (sin -ED)

Ejemplos:

  • Did you work yesterday?
  • Did she study last night?
  • Did they play soccer?
  • Did he arrive on time?

Respuestas cortas:

  • Yes, I did. / No, I didn't.
  • Yes, she did. / No, she didn't.
  • Yes, they did. / No, they didn't.

🧠 Quiz: Forma Interrogativa

¿Cuál es la pregunta correcta?

🕐 Expresiones de Tiempo Pasado

Con pasado simple usamos:

📅 Tiempo específico:

  • Yesterday (ayer)
  • Last night (anoche)
  • Last week (la semana pasada)
  • Last month (el mes pasado)
  • Last year (el año pasado)

⏰ Tiempo exacto:

  • At 3 o'clock (a las 3)
  • In 2020 (en 2020)
  • On Monday (el lunes)
  • This morning (esta mañana)
  • Two days ago (hace dos días)

📚 Verbos Regulares Comunes

🏠 Actividades diarias:

  • cook → cooked (cocinar)
  • clean → cleaned (limpiar)
  • wash → washed (lavar)
  • brush → brushed (cepillar)
  • finish → finished (terminar)

🎓 Estudio y trabajo:

  • study → studied (estudiar)
  • work → worked (trabajar)
  • learn → learned (aprender)
  • practice → practiced (practicar)
  • help → helped (ayudar)

🎮 Entretenimiento:

  • play → played (jugar)
  • watch → watched (ver)
  • listen → listened (escuchar)
  • dance → danced (bailar)
  • visit → visited (visitar)

🎭 Diálogo de Ejemplo

🗣️ Hablando del fin de semana:

A: What did you do last weekend?

B: I visited my grandmother on Saturday.

A: That's nice! Did you stay there all day?

B: Yes, I did. We cooked together and watched old movies.

A: Sounds fun! What about Sunday?

B: I studied for my English exam and cleaned my room.

A: Did you finish everything?

B: Yes, I finished all my homework.

🎯 Quiz: Comprensión del diálogo

Según el diálogo, ¿qué hizo B el sábado?
  • Studied for an exam
  • Visited his grandmother
  • Cleaned his room
  • Worked all day

🔄 Diferencia: Presente vs Pasado

Comparación:

🔄 Presente Simple:

  • I work every day
  • She studies English
  • Do you play soccer?
  • We don't watch TV

⏰ Pasado Simple:

  • I worked yesterday
  • She studied last night
  • Did you play soccer?
  • We didn't watch TV

⚠️ Errores Comunes

🚫 Evita estos errores:

  • ❌ I didn't worked → ✅ I didn't work
  • ❌ Did you worked? → ✅ Did you work?
  • ❌ She studyed → ✅ She studied
  • ❌ They stoped → ✅ They stopped
  • ❌ I plaied → ✅ I played
